Understanding Cookies

Cookies are tiny data files that a website places on your computer or mobile device via your web browser. Their primary function is to enable the site to recall your device during future visits, facilitating various operations like tailoring content, maintaining user sessions, and gathering analytical data. These small files are instrumental in delivering a richer and more personalized browsing experience.

Our Application of Cookies

Vasealityoff deploys cookies for several distinct objectives, which encompass but are not restricted to the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are indispensable for the website's fundamental operation, allowing you to move around the site and utilize core functionalities, such as accessing protected sections. Services reliant on these cookies cannot function without them.
  • Analytical/Statistical Cookies: These assist us in comprehending how users engage with our digital platform by gathering and relaying anonymized statistics, thereby enabling ongoing enhancements to our website.
  • Preference Cookies: Employed to identify you upon your return to our platform, these cookies facilitate the retention of your settings and offer refined, individualized functionalities.
  • Marketing/Promotional Cookies: These cookies register your presence on our site, track the specific pages you have viewed, and note the links you have clicked. Their purpose is to present advertisements that align with your presumed interests, thereby enriching your online experience.

Furthermore, you possess the capability to remove any cookies already stored on your device whenever you choose.
